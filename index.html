Case Study 01 · AWS
Enabling Comcast’s First AWS Marketplace Listing
Problem
Comcast had an untapped co-sell opportunity on AWS Marketplace but lacked a clear path to list their DataBee solution and activate partner-led revenue.
Strategy
Identified DataBee as an ideal candidate, mapped internal AWS and Comcast stakeholders, and built a cross-functional plan to move from concept to listing.
Execution
  • Facilitated alignment between Comcast engineering, AWS partner team, and marketplace ops
  • Navigated technical and contractual requirements to clear listing blockers
  • Coordinated launch communications and internal AWS enablement
Outcome: Comcast’s first-ever AWS Marketplace listing — opening a new co-sell channel and strengthening the partnership.
Case Study 02 · Veeam
Scaling a National Partner Program to President’s Club
Problem
Veeam’s National Partner program needed stronger channel activation to hit an aggressive $4.1M annual quota across a distributed partner network.
Strategy
Prioritized top-tier partners by deal velocity and enablement readiness, then built co-sell motions and incentive programs to accelerate pipeline.
Execution
  • Ran partner business reviews (QBRs) focused on pipeline and KPIs, not activity
  • Designed joint go-to-market plays with top 5 channel partners
  • Enabled partner reps through field training and deal coaching
  • Aligned with marketing on demand-gen campaigns targeted at partner verticals
Outcome: 110% of $4.1M quota — Americas Channel MVP and President’s Club 2017.
Case Study 03 · Flexera
Launching 6 Cloud Marketplace Offerings in 9 Months
Problem
Flexera had no established marketplace presence on AWS or Azure, limiting co-sell opportunities and partner-sourced pipeline.
Strategy
Mapped Flexera’s product portfolio to marketplace eligibility criteria, identified the highest-ROI listings, and built an internal process to move from zero to live.
Execution
  • Collaborated with product and legal to satisfy marketplace technical requirements
  • Built relationships with AWS and Azure partner teams to accelerate approvals
  • Trained internal sales on co-sell motions for each new listing
Outcome: 6 live marketplace listings (2 AWS, 4 Azure) — establishing Flexera’s cloud co-sell foundation. Revenue pace: 68% of $1.6M target in 9 months.
